pov

What is love beside baby don't hurt me, don't hurt me, no more?

Taking form of an interactive non-linear short film, pov is a reflection on love and identity. The audiences are invited on a first date with Nhan, where they talk to Nhan through a video call, and get to know him through a series of choices. Based on their decisions, Nhan tells different stories of his life. All eventually reach the climax inviting the audiences to get into the room with him.

pov is Nhan’s reflection on online dating, where he must constantly edit, refine, and re-introduce himself; again, and again, and again. At the end of the day, who is Nhan? Can a digital avatar ever fully represent Nhan? Or are all these versions simply parallel choices, running beside one another without ever fully merging?
