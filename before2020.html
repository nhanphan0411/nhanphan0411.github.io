(2019) 0 → 100

0→100 An open art and economics experiment.

CHALLENGE Create anything, in any medium, for under 100.000VND, or about $5. Keep your receipts. (Note that we don't count time spent or existing tools like computers. Don't overthink it.)

RESULT The artworks are auctioned off after the exhibition. (The highest bid for a 100.000VND artwork was 3.000.000VND, or a 30x return on creativity.)

MERCHANDISE Besides the artwork, we created merchandise and sold it at cost (all under 100.000VND)

100.000VND CAN'T BUY A LOT. 100.000VND CAN BUY A LOT. DO YOU NEED TO BUY?
